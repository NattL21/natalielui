The Problem

Many Gen-Z college students struggle to stay motivated and organized when managing daily tasks, often leading to tasks piling up. This common issue results in stress and burnout, especially amongst students.

Existing services are often too convoluted or uninspiring, lacking intuitive organization and features that encourage sustained engagement.

Our Solution

Our team set out to design a task management mobile application that helps students balance their responsibilities through streamlined organization and gamification engagement centered around the rising popularity in collecting blind boxes. We wanted to create an interface that was clear but preserved the features we hoped to include such as a pomodoro timer and a collaborative aspect that allowed users to work with others. While these features helped make our app well rounded, we still wanted the core purpose of the app - task management - to be streamlined and efficient for the user.
